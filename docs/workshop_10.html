For this workshop we are going to spend 40 minutes each on making p5.js sketches that include the following libraries:
  1. RiTa.js
  2. p5.gui
For the next 40 minutes, let's take a look at RiTa.js by Daniel Howe. Let's have a look at the collection of examples on the p5.js online editor (don't forget to show all complete file structure Joel!)and also some of the projects that have been made with RiTa.js.
Helpfully, Allison Parrish has posted a great tutorial on how to use external JavaScript libraries with p5.js (with rita.js as the example library), let's go through it together and get it going in the p5.js editor.
For the final 40 minutes, let's put everything together by duplicating some of the p5.gui examples from GitHub, adding them to a new editor.p5js sketch, and coming up with some new things to do with our GUI.
